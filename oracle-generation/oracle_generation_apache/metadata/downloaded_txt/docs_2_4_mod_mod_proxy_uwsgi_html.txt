    at least one load balancer algorithm module, such as mod_lbmethod_byrequests , in addition to the proxy

    default, and will be used for this example configuration. Balanced gateway ProxyPass "/uwsgi-bin/" "balancer://somecluster/"

    BalancerMember uwsgi://localhost:4001
